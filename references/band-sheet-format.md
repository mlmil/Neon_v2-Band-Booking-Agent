# The Band Sheet Format

> ⚠️ **IMPORTANT**: This reference mirrors the authoritative format specified in SKILL.md. The SKILL.md is the source of truth — if anything conflicts, SKILL.md wins. The formatting documented here is the format Mike expects in GroupMe.

## Overview

The Band Sheet is a weekly scheduling summary posted to the Neon Blonde GroupMe. It has four sections in a fixed order:
1. BOOKED GIGS
2. MEMBERS OUT
3. FULLY FREE WEEKENDS
4. OPEN DAYS

## Exact Template

```
NEON BLONDE - THE BAND SHEET
Updated: [Month Day, Year]

---

BOOKED GIGS:

SAT MARCH 15 @ 3pm — Parquee 1055 (Santa Paula)
FRI MARCH 20 @ 8pm — The Sewer (Ventura)
SAT MARCH 28 @ 4pm — Fox Wine Company

---

MEMBERS OUT:

- Alfred: March 13
- Curtis: April 18
- Dave: May 9

---

FULLY FREE WEEKENDS:
(No gigs + all members available)

- SAT-SUN MARCH 21-22
- SAT-SUN MAY 30-31

---

OPEN DAYS:
(Single days available - no gigs, all members free)

- SAT MARCH 14
- SUN MARCH 29
- SUN APRIL 5

---

Questions? Hit me up. - Neon
```

## Formatting Rules

### Date Format
- **MANDATORY**: `SAT MARCH 15` or `SUN APRIL 12`
- NOT: "Saturday, March 15" or "March 15" or "3/15"
- Day-of-week abbreviation (SAT/SUN/FRI/etc) + FULL MONTH NAME + day number
- For ranges: `SAT-SUN MARCH 21-22`

### Time Format
- **MANDATORY**: `@ 3pm` or `@ 8pm`
- NOT: "at 3pm" or "3:00pm" or "15:00"
- Always use @ symbol, lowercase am/pm, no minutes unless necessary

### Venue Format
- **MANDATORY**: `@ The Sewer (Ventura)` or `@ Leashless (Ventura)`
- Include city in parentheses for clarity

### Section Headers
- ALL CAPS with colon
- BOOKED GIGS:
- MEMBERS OUT:
- FULLY FREE WEEKENDS:
- OPEN DAYS:

### Only Future Events
- Filter out ALL past events (anything before today's date)
- The Band Sheet is forward-looking only

### Member Out Format
- Bullet list: `- [Name]: [Date or Date Range]`
- Example: `- Alfred: March 13` or `- Dave: May 9-June 28`

## Four Sections (in order)

1. **BOOKED GIGS** — Future gigs only, chronological, with date/time/venue
2. **MEMBERS OUT** — Future unavailability only, bullet list format
3. **FULLY FREE WEEKENDS** — Complete weekends (Sat-Sun) with NO gigs and ALL members available
4. **OPEN DAYS** — Individual days (single Fri/Sat/Sun) that are open (no gig, all members free)

⚠️ **Critical**: The Band Sheet website's "Weekend Days Open" list only shows days without gigs. It does NOT check member availability. Always cross-reference against calendar member-out events before calling a day "open."

## When to Update

Post a new Band Sheet:
- After a new gig is confirmed
- When a member adds/changes unavailability
- When a gig is cancelled
- Weekly on a regular schedule (suggest: Sunday evening for the coming week)

## Copy-Paste Process

After generating the Band Sheet:
1. Copy the entire formatted text
2. Paste into Neon Blonde GroupMe
3. Delete the previous Band Sheet from GroupMe notes to avoid confusion
4. Confirm: "Band Sheet updated"
